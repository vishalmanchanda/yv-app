Vision

To empower India's future by bridging ancient Indian Knowledge Systems (IKS) with cutting-edge AI, fostering innovation that benefits society.

Mission

  • Democratize IKS

    Make IKS accessible to all through an engaging AI-powered platform built using our curated datasets on GitHub.

  • Drive Innovation

    Host biannual hackathons on January 12 (Swami Vivekananda's Birth Anniversary) and June 21 (International Day of Yoga) to explore modern applications of IKS.

  • Cultivate an AI/ML Ecosystem

    Build a community of innovators leveraging IKS for impactful solutions.

  • Create Ethical AI

    Develop AI/ML tools that align with IKS values and address societal challenges.

  • Inspire IKS Literacy

    Cultivate a generation that appreciates and applies IKS principles in their lives.
